About the Event

✦

"The sustainer of this universe is knocking at the gates of your heart, trying to enter through the portals of silence and make within you a garden of happiness."

— Sri Sri Paramahansa Yogananda

Each year sannyasis of Yogoda Satsanga Society of India visit cities around the country to help fulfil the ever-growing demand for Paramahansa Yogananda’s soul-liberating teachings. These monastic tours offer a blessed opportunity for newcomers who wish to introduce themselves to the teachings of Paramahansa Yogananda and receive in-depth guidance in the YSS meditation techniques for YSS Lessons students.

As every spiritual event gives a new stimulus to one’s sadhana, your participation in these events will rekindle your spiritual enthusiasm, and will also help you to experience the joy and blessings that come from meditating with and being in the company of other earnest seekers.

We invite you to join these events and withdraw your attention from the ceaseless activities of everyday living and focus it on the Inner Silence, and thereby drink the nectar of God’s peace and bliss. During your participation, you may also avail yourself of the guidance of YSS sannyasis in the application of Gurudeva’s teachings in your daily life.
